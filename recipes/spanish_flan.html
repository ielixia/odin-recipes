Spanish Flan

"Flan" can refer to a couple different things depending on where you live. In this case, it's a baked custard dessert topped with caramel.

Flan Ingredients

These are the five ingredients you'll need to make this top-rated flan recipe:
  • 1 cup of white sugar: A cup of white sugar is melted on the stove until it's liquified and golden.
  • 3 large eggs: You'll need three whole eggs.
  • 1 can (14 ounce) of sweetened condensed milk: Because all the most delicious recipes call for a can of sweetened condensed milk.
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) of evaporated milk: This gives the flan a richer flavor than regular milk.
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract:It will enhance the overall flavor of the Spanish flan.

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Melt sugar in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat until liquefied and golden in color. Carefully pour hot syrup into a 9-inch round glass baking dish, turning the dish to evenly coat the bottom and sides; set aside.
  3. Beat eggs in a large bowl. Add condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla; beat until smooth. Pour egg mixture into the baking dish; cover with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour. Let cool completely.
  5. To serve, carefully invert onto a rimmed serving plate; let caramel sauce flow over flan.
